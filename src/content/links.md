## A note on staying current

AI tools move fast. Model names, features, and even doc URLs change. The habits in this guide, managing context, verifying work, being specific, are durable. The exact commands may drift. When in doubt, check the official docs and the built-in `/help`.

That is the whole guide. Go build something, and come back any time you need a refresher.
